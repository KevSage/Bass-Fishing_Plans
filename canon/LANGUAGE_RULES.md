# LANGUAGE & CLARITY RULES (LOCKED)

These rules apply to ALL user-facing text in BFP and AIQ.

---

## Clarifier Rules

- Clarifiers explain a concept ONCE
- Never stack clarifiers in a single sentence
- Space clarifiers across the paragraph
- Vary sentence length deliberately
- Avoid glossary or teaching tone

---

## Rhythm Rules

- No two consecutive sentences should share:
  - Length
  - Structure
  - Entry angle

- Expound context when necessary to vary cadence

---

## Tone Rules

- Calm
- Grounded
- Professional
- Confident without hype

---

## Prohibited

- Bullet-like sentence repetition
- Mechanical definitions
- Over-explaining fundamentals
- Marketing language inside plans
