# PRESENTATION FAMILY CANON (LOCKED)

All patterns are selected at the presentation family level.
This canon applies to BOTH BFP and AnglerIQ.

---

## The 7 Universal Presentation Families

1. **Surface Chase**
   - Continuous surface motion
   - Examples: Whopper Plopper, Buzzbait, Wakebait

2. **Surface Ambush**
   - Stop-start or precision surface work
   - Examples: Frog, Popper, Walking Bait

3. **Horizontal Moving**
   - Constant mid-water motion
   - Examples: Crankbait, Chatterbait, Spinnerbait

4. **Slow-Glide Horizontal**
   - Subtle, non-chase movement
   - Examples: Glidebait, Soft Jerkbait

5. **Bottom Contact — Dragging**
   - Crawling across bottom
   - Examples: Texas Rig (drag), Football Jig, Carolina Rig

6. **Bottom Contact — Lift / Hop**
   - Lift, hop, or stroke presentations
   - Examples: Jig hopping, Shaky Head, Blade Yo-Yo

7. **Vertical / Hover**
   - Minimal horizontal movement
   - Examples: Dropshot, Damiki, Ned Rig

---

## Locked Rules

- A pattern belongs to ONE family only
- Alternates must remain within the same family
- Families are implicit in BFP, explicit in AIQ
