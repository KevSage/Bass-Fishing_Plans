# GEAR MATRIX BY PRESENTATION FAMILY (LOCKED)

Gear is dictated by presentation family.
Gear is NOT dynamically tuned beyond contextual mention.

---

## Surface Chase / Surface Ambush
- Rod: 7’–7’4” MH Casting
- Reel: 7.3–8.1:1
- Line: Braid

## Horizontal Moving
- Rod: 7’–7’3” MH Casting
- Reel: 6.8–7.5:1
- Line: Fluorocarbon (or braid to leader in grass)

## Bottom Contact — Dragging
- Rod: 7’1”–7’5” H Casting
- Reel: 7.1–8.1:1
- Line: Fluorocarbon or braid to leader

## Bottom Contact — Lift / Hop
- Rod: 7’ MH–H Casting
- Reel: 7.1:1
- Line: Fluorocarbon

## Vertical / Hover (Finesse)
- Rod: 6’10”–7’2” M/MH Spinning
- Reel: 2500–3000
- Line: Light braid to fluorocarbon leader

---

## Locked Rules

- Gear is suggested, not tuned
- Refinements are contextual, not adjustable menus
- Technique dictates gear, not vice versa
